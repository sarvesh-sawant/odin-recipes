Vada Pav Recipe

Description

Burger bole toh bakwaas vabapav bole toh jhakaas

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Onion
  • Masala
  • Salt
  • Pav
  • Gram flour

Steps

  1. Boil Potato
  2. Cut onion
  3. Mash Potatos
  4. Add Masala and Salt to Mash Potatos
  5. Create balls of above mixture
  6. Coat with Gram flour
  7. Deep fry
  8. Server with Pav
  9. Enjoy
