Pav Bhaji Recipe

Description

Keep calm and eat Pav Bhaji

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Pav Bhaji Masala
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Boil Potato and Tomatoes
  2. Mash boiled potato and tomatoes
  3. Cook Mashed potato and tomatoes
  4. Add Pav Bhaji Masala and salt
  5. Serve Pav Bhaji with cut lemon and cut onions
  6. Enjoy
