Omelette Recipe

Description

You can eat an Omelette at midnight, at lunchtime, all day long. It's perfect for every occasion.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Onion
  • Masala
  • Salt
  • Cut chillis
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Beat Egg
  2. Add cut onion, masala, salt, chilli and mix it
  3. Cook to preference
  4. Enjoy
